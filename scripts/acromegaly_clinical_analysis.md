Correlation with BMI
-----------------------




The BMI significantly correlated with the natural logarithm of the BMI (p=0.0139, r=0.554, R2=0.3069)To correct for the BMI effect on the HOMA-IR score, I generated a linear model comparing the HOMA score to the BMI and the diagnosis.  We tested for an interaction between HOMA-IR and BMI in this model, and did not observe any evidence of an interaction (p=0.7533).
